Privacy Policy for EZ HIJAB

Good Guise International is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how our mobile application, EZ HIJAB ("the App"), collects, uses, and safeguards information related to your use of the app. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. No Data Collection

We respect your privacy and are proud to inform you that EZ HIJAB collects no user data. This means we do not gather, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII), usage statistics, or metadata from your use of the App. Your photos, data, and activity remain private and are stored solely on your device.

2. Permissions

EZ HIJAB requires the following permissions for its core functionality:

  • Camera Access: The App requests permission to access your device's camera to allow you to take photos that you can edit with the app.
  • Photo Gallery (Read-Only Access): The App requests permission to access your device’s photo gallery in read-only mode. This access allows you to select existing photos from your gallery to add hijabs to them. The App does not modify, delete, or otherwise interact with files beyond the selected photo for editing.

3. How We Handle Your Photos

All photo editing takes place on your device locally. The photos you edit or create using EZ HIJAB are not uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

As EZ HIJAB does not collect data, the App does not integrate with or rely on any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or user tracking purposes.

5. Children’s Privacy

EZ HIJAB is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not collect personal information from children. As the App does not collect data, there is no risk of unauthorized data collection from minors.

6. Security

While we do not collect any data, we encourage you to keep your device secure and follow general best practices to protect your personal information and data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. When we update the Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top. Your continued use of EZ HIJAB after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.
